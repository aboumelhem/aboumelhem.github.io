Welcome to the Metal Gear Timeline
1918 - The Philosophers, an organisation that aims to control everything ranging from economics, politics and power, is created from joint forces of China, Russia and USA. It isn't known who the Philosophers are.
1939 - The Philosophers made a pact during World War 2 that leads to the creation of "The Philosophers Legacy". This legacy contains the names of the secret members in the organisation and one hundred billion dollars.

1962 - The CIA creates a unit called FOX (Force Operation X), a group used to undertake special secret operations. It is led by a man named Major Zero. Another military grouped named XOF created by Major Zero was created to provide tactical support for FOX. A man named Skull Face leads the group.
1964 - The CIA sends their best agent The Boss to Russia in an attempt to find the Philosophers Legacy.
